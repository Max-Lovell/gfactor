In this task, you will be shown a sequence of digits, one at a time. You will then be presented with a single string of numbers, and will be asked to decide if these are the same sequence as the numbers previously shown one-by-one. On some trials, the string of numbers will have one digit changed. The length of this string of numbers will change throughout the task.

The first 20 trials are practice trials with feedback. The subsequent 50 trials will be ask you to rate your confidence in your response. Following this, the task will switch to a second variant, again with 20 practice and 50 confidence trials. Please try to use the entire confidence scale throughout the task if you can.
